Zebra Cake

Meet the Zebra Cake

This zebra cake is an easy-to-make icebox cake. It seems to be as popular today as it was 50 years ago.

ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 (9 ounce) package chocolate wafer cookies
  • ¼ cup grated chocolate

Steps to make this a reality

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer. Gradually add confectioners' sugar and vanilla extract, continuing to beat until stiff peaks form.
  3. Spread a generous teaspoon of whipped cream onto each cookie.Spread a generous teaspoon of whipped cream onto each cookie.
  4. Press cookies together to make 3-inch stacks.
  5. Spread whipped cream in a 1-inch-wide line down the center of a serving platter. Turn cookie stacks sideways and join into a log; place in the whipped cream to secure
  6. Frost cookie log with remaining whipped cream and sprinkle with grated chocolate. Cover tightly and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight. Frost cookie log with remaining whipped cream and sprinkle with grated chocolate. Cover tightly and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight.
  7. To serve, slice diagonally to create striped pieces
